8 /*!
9  * \ingroup GeneralRelativityGroup
10  * \brief Raises or lowers the first index of a rank 3 tensor which is symmetric
11  * in the last two indices.
12  *
13  * \details If \f$T_{abc}\f$ is a tensor with \f$T_{abc} = T_{acb}\f$ and the
14  * indices \f$a,b,c,...\f$ can represent either spatial or spacetime indices,
15  * then the tensor \f$ T^a_{bc} = g^{ad} T_{abc} \f$ is computed, where \f$
16  * g^{ab}\f$ is the inverse metric, which is either a spatial or spacetime
17  * metric. If a tensor \f$ S^a_{bc} \f$ is passed as an argument than the
18  * corresponding tensor \f$ S_{abc} \f$ is calculated with respect to the metric
19  * \f$g_{ab}\f$.
20  */
